Our Story

Luigi's brings you the best of fresh ingredients and authentic original recipes from the Mediterranean along with an ambience of a unique cultural heritage. With a wide array of fresh salads, soups, pasta, pizza and grills it is a personification of 'the chilled out ambience'.

It was the summer of 2018 when Dan and Frank Carney decided to open a pizzeria. With mouth-watering pizzas prepared by the founders themselves, Luigi's soon became the most popular neighbourhood restaurant. From then to over 13,200 restaurants across the world today, we have come a long way.
